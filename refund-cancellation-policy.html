Refund/Cancellation Policy

Our commitment to providing quality training with clear policies

Important Notice

All sales of our online courses and IT training programs are final. We do not offer refunds or cancellations under any circumstances.

Refund/Cancellation Policy

At Tekksol Global, we are committed to providing high-quality online courses and IT training. Please be aware that we have a strict no refund policy.

No Refund Policy

All sales of our online courses and IT training programs are final. Once a purchase is made, we do not offer refunds or cancellations under any circumstances.

Why We Have This Policy

Our no-refund policy is in place because:

  • Course materials and resources are made available immediately upon enrollment
  • We invest significant resources in course development and instructor allocation
  • This allows us to maintain competitive pricing for all our students
  • It ensures fair treatment for all enrolled students

Before You Enroll

We encourage all prospective students to:

  • Carefully review course descriptions and requirements
  • Check the course curriculum and learning objectives
  • Ensure you meet the technical requirements for online courses
  • Contact our support team with any questions before enrolling

Course Access

Upon successful enrollment and payment, you will receive immediate access to:

  • All course materials and resources
  • Learning management system access
  • Instructor support channels
  • Any included certifications or assessments
